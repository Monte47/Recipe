Thai Curry

Description

Thai curry dish is made from curry paste, coconut milk or water, meat, seafood, vegetables or fruit, and herbs. Wikipedia History

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked jasmine rice
  • 1/2 cup green/red curry paste, quality authentic paste is key
  • 1-2 cans (13.5) ounce coconut cream
  • beef/chicken broth
  • 2 chicken breasts, thinly sliced (or protein of your choice)
  • 3 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 1/4 cup palm or coconut sugar
  • 2-3 shoots lemongrass
  • 1/2 onion, choped
  • 2 teaspoons garlic
  • pineapple, cubed
  • 2-4 thai chiles, chopped
  • 3 tsp lime juice
  • fresh kaffir lime leaves
  • snow peas
  • peppers, chopped
  • various vegetables of your choice, usually carrots and peas
  • 2 tsp garlic
  • 3 tsp ginger
    •

    Directions

    1. In a large pot, lightly fry your garlic and curry powder in some oil until it opens up well, do not let it burn.
    2. Add your coconut cream, stir consistently and add broth until consitency is to your taste. Crack/pound and add lemongrass shoots. Heat to a light boil.
    3. Add more curry paste to taste. Add protein and heavy vegetables. Stir until cooked lightly. If your chicken is cooked through, you have gone too long. Add thai chiles.
    4. Reduce heat. Add lighter vegetables, coconut sugar, fish sauce, lime juice, and ginger. Let your curry lightly simmer for a bit.
    5. Remove from heat. Add pineapple and lime leaves. Stir well. Let sit for a while to thicken. Remove lemongrass. Serve over rice. Garnish with cilantro or thai basil.
